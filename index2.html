Biography

Addicted to Worship, Worship Addicts is a team of men and women dedicated to perpetuating worship music, fellowship and a heartbeat that attracts and retains God's presence, changes atmospheres and sharpens the church of Jesus Christ to reverence Him only. Not just music but divine atmospheres at home, at work, at school, on the road, in cities and nations.... .

The Worship Addicts Devotional Series is a package of pure worship music that carries a presence. After years of personal and corporate worship experiences in southern Africa, the divine walk led to composing over 500 songs, (and still counting, most of them by Takesure Zamar Ncube) , sung across denominations, in informal groups, small & large congregations, in different versions and settings, as there had been no official digital releases. Thus, the journey to systematically encode and distribute it to the Body of Christ began in the second decade of the new millennium.

With the release of Worship Addicts Devotional Volume 1 in June 2011, the resounding success was followed by Devotional Volume 2 in August 2012.... history is in the making. With a minimum of at least two "worship only" albums per year, more and more people are realising the potency of worship in their daily lives. Thus said, the tendency for this music to naturally appeal to the "mature" or focused Christians cannot be ignored....and you cannot ignore the music either, so go ahead, be part of the worship e-xperience.
