Music In The Kingdom

Music has always been and will remain instrumental in the Kingdom of God. From the fall of Lucifer to the projected last trumpet of eschatological times, right through Old Testament Israel to contemporary gospel, music has been a silent influence. We all are under its influence in life whether we are aware of it or not. Many used it, yet others have misused it. Religious and non religious people alike, kings and clergy, revolutionaries and reformers like martin Luther have been inspired. However, the roots of music in the kingdom are firmly rooted in the power of God. Joshua and Israel blew trumpets to bring down the walls of Jericho, Saul used it to drive out demons out of His life, David became a worshiper, a man after God's own heart, hence an open heaven and an established Kingdom- one that lasts forever. Even the Lord Jesus sung a hymn with His disciples after the resurrection, upon his return shall be the voice of trumpet too, notwithstanding angels and saints that minister to Him daily .

Paul encourages us to fulfill the mission of ministering to God and to one another by using music....-sing hymns and spiritual songs. (Loving God and neighbor Matthew 22 v36-40)

" And when they had sung an hymn, they went out into the mount of Olives." Jesus and His Desciples, Matthew 26:30:

"Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching and admonishing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to the Lord"
